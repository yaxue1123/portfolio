September 2017 - December 2017

As a group of four, we tested four students at UNC on three tasks using the sakai calendar tool: add event, link calendar, and view deadlines; and we collected qualitative and quantitative data through the think-aloud approach, surveys and interviews according to evaluation measures including UX Goal, UX Measures and UX Metrics. then brought up three main recommendations for Sakai calendar based on the evaluation result for further improvement.
