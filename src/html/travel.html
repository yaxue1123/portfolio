Spring Break 2018

I went to Las Vegas, Death Valley, Grand Canyon, Horseshoe Bend and Antelope Canyon this time with two friends, across four states including Nevada, California, Arizona and Corolado. This one-week trip gave me a lot of unforgettable memories.

This is a night view of Vegas from the top of Rio Hotel. We were waiting for the zip line riding. Can you see the high roller with green lights?

I jumped really high this time at Badwater Basin in the Death Valley National Park! This is the lowest point in North America as 86 meters below the sea level.

Winter Break 2017

I spent a few days of my last winter holiday in Florida with five friends to embrace the warmth of the southest state of the U.S. We flied to Miami, then drove to Key West and Orlando, then flied back from Orlando at last.

Our first stop at Miami was seafoods from Bubba Gump Shrimp! This is a resturant inspired by the film Forrest Gump. See the bench and the shoes I "wore".
