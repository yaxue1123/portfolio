Basketball

I started playing basketball in junior high school, just playing with my friends and having fun. Then I got chances to play school-level games in undergraduate school, and played center in staight four years. Basketball teaches me persistence, passion and collaboration. I also played a few games in UNC, and still practice once in a while. I love basketball so much.

This was my freshman year in CUFE School of Information Women's Basketball Team. I was arranged for the first team, playing center.

April 2014

Second year for "CUFE CUP", we still didn't make through top 8 teams. However, practice makes perfect. My personal skills as well as teamwork leveled up a lot.

April 2015

Third year for "CUFE CUP", and second year for my caption position. We hired more team members and built a big family of iSchool Women's Basketball team.

April 2016

Fourth year, also the last year. How time flies! More and more new team members came in and we were upon graduation. I will miss you girls so much.

April 2017

Three of my best friends in undergraduate school. We became friends because of basketball, and played together for three years.

July 2017

My first game in UNC vs Wake Forest. We lost the game but the practice really improved our collabortion. I expect to play more games in the future!
